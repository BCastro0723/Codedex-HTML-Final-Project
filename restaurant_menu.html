Beverages

Berry Bliss - $3.99

Got a sweet tooth on the job? We got you covered! Our Berry Bliss is hand-shaken with a mix of cranberry juice as well as natural blackberry flavoring and real fruit. A tastful reward for a hard days work!

Cucumber Lemon Lime Water - $1.99

Just what our local cowboys and cowgirls need after a hard days work. Our straight-from-the-well water comes out ice cold and fresh from Mother Nature. Our locally sourced produce allows us to mix your bevereage with the freshest cucumbers, lemons, and limes in the Panhandle. What a refreshment!

Fruity Fizz - $3.99

Do you remember a time when you were young when you were playing outside, and all you wanted to cool off with is a nice glass of freshly-squeezed lemonade? If yes, we've taken it to the next level! Our Fizz brings together all the plesantries of freshly-squeezed lemonade with our own locally grown strawberries and mint leaves to bring you a Texas-Tornado of fruity refreshment. If no, you'll love the drink anyways!

Shareables

Mozzarella Sticks - $9.99

A 6 piece dish of deliciousness! Our hand-breaded mozzarella sticks are deep fried, seasoned with freshly chopped parsley flakes, and served with our house-made marinara sauce. Or our house-made ranch. It's your choice!

Fried Pickles - $7.99

Who doesn't love a dill pickle after a long day of herding cattle? Our hand-battered fried pickles are the perfect end to a hard-day's work as well as the perfect starter to a country-style dinner. Topped with freshly copped parsely flakes, our pickles are served with our signature house-made ranch or our house-made cajun horseradish (not made with real horses).

Onion Blossom - $8.99

They say everything is bigger in Texas... AND EVERYTHING IS. Including our local-favorite - the Onion Blossom. This large onion might be harder to lasso than a wild horse! Our deep fried favorite can be served with your choice of house-made ranch. Or if you're feeling wildly western... Spicy Ranch!

House Salad - $4.99

Tired from herding cattle all day long and not that hungry? No problem! Our House Salad is the perfect solution to nourish your slight hunger while keeping you healthy and strong for herding those big mooers!

Caesar Salad - $4.99

We don't know who Caesar is, but he sure makes a good salad! Our Caesar Salad comes served with ingredients from our local farmer's market with our house-baked croutons to top it off. Nothing like helping the local community and enjoying a fine, healthy snack afterward.
